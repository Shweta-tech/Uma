Unnat Maharashtra Abhiyan

Connecting Higher and Technical Education with Development Sector


------------ Vision Mission & Objectives ------------

  • Vision

    Its vision is to build an independent and public knowledge infrastructure for the state of Maharashtra which will bring socio-economic and cultural development for its people, especially those in the bottom 80% of the socio-economic strata.

    Mission

    Its mission is to-

    (i) enable demand-driven research: enable citizens, elected representatives, and state agencies to access institutions of learning (i.e., institutions of higher education, IHEs) and for topical research such as in feasibility studies, evaluation, assessment, or innovative design.

    (ii) align with development: to align curricula and research in higher education towards better development outcomes and to train students for a future role as development professionals and researchers.

    • Objectives

    Its objectives include-

    (i) To align curricula and research with regional development needs, train students for a future role as development professionals and researchers and strengthen their capabilities in real-life problem areas.

    (ii) To re-emphasize field-work and case-studies as an important pedagogical tool within research and academia.

    (iii) To create avenues for students and faculty members to work on live problems.

    (iv) To provide a formal mechanism for local bodies (such as Zilla Parishads, Gram Panchayats, etc.) and the public at large to access institutes of higher education for their knowledge needs.
