    Welcome to Unnat Maharashtra Abhiyan

    Unnat Maharashtra Abhiyan (UMA) is an institutional framework conceptualized and operated jointly by CTARA, IIT Bombay and Government of Maharashtra, India. It envisages the creation of a network of trusted higher education institutions in the state to support its development agenda. It is currently being rolled out in engineering colleges and polytechnics across the state of Maharashtra through a set of enabling Government Resolutions (GRs)

    About UMA

    UMA seeks to build an independent knowledge infrastructure in the public domain for the state of Maharashtra which will bring socio-economic and cultural development for its people, especially those in the bottom 80% of the socio-economic strata.

    Workshops and Events

    Workshops are regularly organised on various themes. The theme for 2019-2020 was electricity and micro, small and medium enterprises. These workshops develop expertise for UMA institutions in new areas and also produce resource material which may be used in course-work.

    TDSC

    Technology and Development Solutions Cell is a resource center for providing technology-based solutions for the development sector. It provides resources and services to undertake projects that deliver outputs via academic research outputs, consulting, outreach modules, entrepreneurship guidance, etc.
    TDSC not only aims at delivering solutions to external stakeholders but primarily uses the academic platform to engage students directly in development-related problems and solutions.
    The resources of the center can be leveraged by faculty and students of IIT Bombay and other colleges to provide solutions to the community and various development stakeholders.
